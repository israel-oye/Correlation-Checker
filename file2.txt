Swift ni a bi ni Amarillo, Texas. O bẹrẹ sise bi ọmọ ni Amarillo Theatre School fun Children. Ṣaaju ọdun giga rẹ ni ile-iwe giga, Swift fi Amarillo silẹ lati lọ si ile-iṣẹ Interlochen fun Arts ni Michigan. Swift lẹhinna lọ si Ile-ẹkọ giga ti Ipinle ti New York ni Ra, lẹhinna gbe ni Ilu New York.

Swift ni awọn ipa ni ọpọlọpọ awọn ere lati 1997 si 2002. Ni ọdun 1997, Swift ṣe irawọ pẹlu Amy Ryan ati Peter Dinklage ni Marking, awada dudu nipasẹ Patrick Breen. Ni ọdun 1998, Swift ni ipa asiwaju ninu Orin Whale, ere nipasẹ Anthony Minghella. Ni ọdun 2000, Swift ṣe irawọ ni Tooth, iṣelọpọ itage ni New York. Ni ọdun 2002, o ṣe irawọ ninu ere Richard Greenberg, The Dazzle, lẹgbẹẹ Peter Frechette.

Lori Ọdọmọbìnrin Gossip, Swift ni ipa loorekoore bi Anne Vanderbilt Archibald, iya ti Nate Archibald. Lati 2010-2011, Swift ṣe Sherrie West, oluranlọwọ agbẹjọro agbegbe lori Ofin & Bere fun: Ẹgbẹ Awọn olufaragba pataki. O ni ipa loorekoore bi Haylie Grimes ni Awọn ode. Ni ọdun 2017, Swift ṣere Cynthia ni Thoroughbreds, eyiti o ṣere ni Sundance Film Festival.
