A mụrụ Swift na Amarillo, Texas. Ọ malitere ime ihe mgbe ọ bụ nwata na Amarillo Theatre School maka ụmụaka. Tupu afọ ọkaibe na ụlọ akwụkwọ sekọndrị, Swift hapụrụ Amarillo ịga Interlochen Center for Arts na Michigan. Swift wee gaa Mahadum State nke New York na Purchase, wee biri na New York City.

Swift nwere ọrụ n'ọtụtụ egwuregwu site na 1997 ruo 2002. N'afọ 1997, Swift gbara aka na Amy Ryan na Peter Dinklage na Marking, ihe ọchị ojii nke Patrick Breen. Na 1998, Swift nwere onye ndu na Whale Music, egwuregwu nke Anthony Minghella. Na 2000, Swift gbara egwu na Tooth, ihe nkiri ihe nkiri na New York. Na 2002, ọ gbara n'egwuregwu Richard Greenberg, The Dazzle, n'akụkụ Peter Frechette.

Na Gossip Girl, Swift nwere ọrụ ugboro ugboro dị ka Anne Vanderbilt Archibald, nne Nate Archibald. Site na 2010-2011, Swift gbara Sherrie West, onye osote onye ọka iwu mpaghara na Iwu & Order: Ngalaba Ndị merụrụ ahụ pụrụ iche. Ọ na-arụ ọrụ ugboro ugboro dị ka Haylie Grimes na ndị ọzọ. Na 2017, Swift gbara Cynthia na Thoroughbreds, nke a na-egwuri na Sundance Film Festival.
